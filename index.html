Sounders Matchday Experience

This story map illustrates the matchday experiences of the Seattle Sounders supporters.

CenturyLink Field

Seattle Sounders FC plays home matches at CenturyLink Field in Seattle, also home to the Seattle Seahawks. CenturyLink Field is a 69,000-seat stadium designed for both teams. The Sounders artificially limit the stadium's capacity for MLS matches, with certain seating sections covered with tarpaulins to provide "a more intimate atmosphere". However, the club does open the entire stadium for international friendly matches and some league matches.

Parking

Parking is available in a variety of areas around Century Link Field. The stadium offers parking close up at the north parking lot and the Century Link Field parking garage. Other close parking areas include the T-Mobile Field parking garage, and other sites, as indicated on the map.

Pregame Food and Drink

No matchday experience is complete without a visit to a pregame location. Finding locations for food and drink outside of the stadium can be a lot of fun as its own experience and also save you a lot of money by staying away from overpried stadium fare. Some of the most popular pregame locations can be found on the map.
